Shark Campaign

Year 2 - Term 1-1

This is the final package design. I used the modules in the background instead of the background colour. The packaging will look like a tiny box, with the banner, string, infographic DIY sheet, stamp, stickers, reference banner and information zinc included inside. I’m playing with the colours The banner has the same triangle shape as one of the modules used. That’s why I picked a triangle as my banner. orange, navy blue, white, and blue. I’m using the same colour pallet for the banner, poster, and everything else. I used orange and blue because it was a shark project and I had chosen reproduction as my topic. The colour orange indicates reproduction, while blue depicts the ocean.

The Irish Box - The untold story

Year 2 - Term 2-1

This video was developed for Samuel Britain, a jewellery designer. The video shows emotions, familial connections, memories, and bonds. It's a kind of cover for a story from the 1960s Irish conflicts. However, the story illustrates how memories, even if they are not pleasant, remain a part of our lives because they are associated with people we care about.
